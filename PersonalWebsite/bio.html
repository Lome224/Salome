About Me

Salome has scored films and worked as an audio director in Toronto, Vancouver, Boston, Los Angeles, and Chicago for films, video games, and exhibitions, with her work featured at international film festivals. She has collaborated with iQiyi director Andy Liu and Zhang Yimou’s post-production team, and composed an award- winning score for the short film Cheatcode. Her classical works have been performed in Toronto, Boston, Vancouver, Beijing, and Moscow.
